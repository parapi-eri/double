Why Parkohu

Parkohu makes parking simpler and faster by helping you send parking SMS messages in just a few taps.

Designed for everyday convenience, the app helps you manage the small but important steps around parking, from sending the correct SMS, to keeping track of your activity, to reminding you before your parking time expires.

Everyday convenience Built for people who use parking by SMS regularly.
Organized flow Vehicles, zones, and history stay easier to manage.
Quick actions Reduce friction when starting a parking session.
Helpful reminders Stay ahead of expiring parking time.

What You Can Do

  • Send parking SMS messages quickly.
  • Get reminded before your parking session expires.
  • Save and manage multiple vehicles.
  • Set and manage parking zones.
  • View the history of sent SMS messages.

Important Notice

Parkohu is built to make the parking process easier, more practical, and more organized, especially for users who frequently use and pay for parking by SMS.
